Quit Smoking
With The Magic Method


Jacob Maimon


Opening | Theoretical | Technical | Contact Me
Opening
When and why?
I developed this method during a 3-year world trip. This is a situation where free time is endless, the offers for intoxicants come from all directions and in all colors, and money is no barrier. I needed a tool that would protect me from this danger. I am sharing this knowledge for the first time. I hope it will be useful to you. This booklet is unique to cigarettes.

The uniqueness of the method.
In all methods that exist for quitting smoking, the idea is that from now on we stop smoking forever. The assumption is that once we have quit smoking, every cigarette we smoke is actually a danger. It's a danger that we will return to smoke as before very quickly. What's the problem here? That the desire to smoke doesn't go away, so every cigarette we smoke from now on, can rekindle the fire very fast.

In our method, the whole approach is opposite. We are not going to stop smoking, we are going to collapse our desire to smoke. According to The Magic Method, you should always smoke as much as you want and whenever you want. But this desire will become so small, that it will no longer be a problem. While implementing the method, you will quite quickly reach smoking up to 3 cigarettes a day, and later up to 5 cigarettes a week. You will not have to make any effort to maintain this dose, as this is the dose you will feel like smoking, and no more. In fact, once your craving for smoking fades, you can smoke freely. Because the small amount you want to smoke this week, is no longer poses a danger: neither in terms of health, nor in terms of returning to smoke as before. You are about to lose your attraction to smoking, and therefore a cigarette here and there is no longer a threat, because it will never end up smoking more than that.
The theoretical part
Why do we smoke?
From the smoker's perspective, for many reasons: he is already used to it, he is addicted to nicotine, it relaxes him, these are his breaks during the day.

All of these things are true, but they are worded very gently. The straight reason for the passion is much more insulting. We smoke because smoking serves us as a shield against this day. What do we need protection from on a daily basis? A lot of bad emotions. Such as: worries, disappointments, frustrations, humiliations, stress of course, and more. Then, facing this emotional suffocation, we need "air". This is the cigarette for a smoker. It's the release of all the emotional suffocation that has accumulated up to it.

The illusion of protection.
What's beauty about a feeling of protection is that it doesn't have to be real, it's enough that we believe in it. In fact, we have become experts at creating fictional defence mechanisms.

Here is a nice example how easy we do it: a few years ago, I gave a lecture in front of a large audience, and the situation caused me a lot of stress. So I took a table and sat down behind it, and started speaking while sitting behind the table. And even though this table that separates me from the audience, provides no real source of protection, I felt much more relaxed. Then I decided to test something: I got up and left the table, and walked freely around the stage, and just imagined that there is a railing at the end of the stage. And you won't believe how smoothly it works: the imagination of the railing that separates me from the audience, did the same job as the table. After all, what does it matter if it's a real table or an imaginary railing, while neither of them has any real protective value?

These are illusions of protection - these are denials. These are fictional defence mechanisms that we choose to believe in, in order to feel protected from some reality.

The smoking is another such illusion of protection.
It's easy to prove that the cigarette is an illusion of protection, and there is no truth in its value, because a cigarette is a very weak substance. A person who smokes on a daily basis, barely feels anything while smoking. So, if smoking is imperceptible, then its emotional value is clearly a fiction. How can cigarettes provide me any protection from anything, if they are not even noticeable? And this is in complete contrast to alcohol and drugs, which are clearly powerful substances. But for these fellows, we have much funnier jokes than about cigarettes. However, today we will focus on smoking...

The cigarette addict's trick.
Once we collapse the illusion of protection of our cigarettes, they immediately lose their appeal. And what is the understanding that we need to assimilate? Simple. Just knowing about cigarettes that we actually smoke nothing. We puff and puff and puff, but no feeling is involved. Once you internalize that you actually smoke something you barely feel, the cigarettes lose their appeal to you. In other words, your addiction collapses.

So far so good, there's just one problem: everyone knows it! Every smoker knows that a cigarette is an imperceptible substance. And yet, he smokes all day long. That is, the cigarettes don't lose their intense appeal to him, even though he knows they are completely powerless.

How does this happen? Because of a mental trick that we do, in order to maintain our fictional shield. We know to say about cigarettes that they are unnoticeable when it's called for, but we have disconnected this knowledge from them - this knowledge is not a connotation attached to them. If when you're smoking a cigarette, you would know to tell yourself that you barely feel it, it would lose its intense appeal to you immediately. However, since this data is not assimilated, a moment later you will forget it completely.

We use the same trick with movies. We can tell that a movie isn't real in general, but we don't know that while we are watching it. If while I am immersed in a thriller, you would tell me about a dead body I am seeing on the screen right now: "You know, this man isn't really dead now, he's just an actor. The man is alive and breathing, and the moment the director says 'CUT!', everyone stands up." How I will react? I will laugh at you of course: "I know that, genius, but why are you ruining the movie for me?"

The problem of problems.
So, we want to attach a new connotation to an object - the cigarette's nothingness to the smoking. What's the problem here? That it's something we don't want to do at all, and everything about it is denied. In fact, we created this lie on purpose, and we preserve it, because that's what creates our desired fictional defence - the cigarettes and smoking that stand as a shield against this day. The million-dollar question: how can we do it? How can we break this illusion of protection, that we deny so strongly and which is so precious to us?

The magic wand.
There's no problem to do it. But in order to do so, we can't use verbal thinking. What's the problem with verbal thinking? Verbal thinking is very unique, because we can think words without their meaning.

Here's an example. You know the situation where you're in some school, it's the end of the day and you're already exhausted, suddenly the teacher switches to dictation and speaks very quickly. What do we do? We write without understanding anything the teacher says. How it happens? The words currently have only a vocal form, without the layer of meaning. So, we can write them, but we can't understand any of it.

We can see the same thing in reading. Sometimes we can read a whole paragraph, while we are bored or just tired, and actually don't understand anything we read. But we read the whole text. That is, we thought all the words, but just without their layer of meaning.

Because linking the nothingness of the cigarette to our smoking is unwanted and denied action, if we will use verbal thinking in order to assimilate it, almost no meaning will emerge. Why would that happen? Because when thinking is denied, the mind immediately undresses the words from their meaning. This is how it protects us from the hateful information. But for our purpose, we must extract the layer of meaning, because this is the understanding, this is how we assimilate the connotation into the object.

That's why we need a magic wand, which is visual thinking. Unlike verbal thinking, which can be thought without meaning, visual thinking always comes with meaning. Therefore, every image we will pop up, always contains its meaning. This way we can tag our smoking to the fact that we actually smoke nothing - through images. That will do the job, and even surprisingly quickly.
The technical part
The chosen technique.
So, we want to convert the point that the cigarette is almost unnoticeable, into a representative image.

There are several ways to do this. My favorite is a pure visual thinking, one that uses imaginary pictures and even short clips. But since this is a booklet, I will go with the easiest way to perform. The effect is the same, but it's a little less colorful and interesting.

We will not use an imaginary picture, but an imaginary shape. The shape is a trick. It lets us use words without losing their meaning. How? We combine words as anchor points within our imaginary shape. Thus, the embedded words are an integral parts of the image. Therefore, we can use them without the danger they will become meaningless very quickly. Let's bring this trick to life.

Performing our image.
First, we need a name for our image: The Puff Triangle. The message of The Puff Triangle: we puff and puff and puff the cigarette, but nothing – there is no feeling.

What does our image look like? It's a simple triangle, with the word "puff" written at its vertex, the words "and puff" written at its right base, and the words "and puff" written at its left base, and the words "and nothing" written inside it.

How do you think it? You move in your imagination on the triangle from point to point. You start at the vertex and say "puff", then you move to the right base and say "and puff", then you move to the left base and say "and puff", and then you go into the center and say "and nothing". You say the words in your heart of course, the words are silent.

Note that the movement on the image from point to point is what keeps the words from losing their meaning. That's why the imaginary movement on the image is necessary. Without the imaginary movement on our triangle, we have actually moved from visual thinking to verbal thinking.

Now you try it. Say: "puff, and puff, and puff, and nothing", while moving in your imagination from point to point across our Puff Triangle. You start on the vertex ("puff"), move to the right base ("and puff"), then to the left base ("and puff"), and end in the center ("and nothing").

If you don't know where to start, just close your eyes. Try it first with your eyes closed. Then do it with your eyes open.

Do this 5-10 times in a row to practice it. Do it also a few times without our Puff Triangle, without the movement - and feel the difference. See how easily you lose all meaning without the movement within our image.

Now we have to link our image to our smoking.
After you have learned to think our Puff Triangle, we move on to the real thing: we attach our image to our smoking. Did you light a cigarette? Is the cigarette in your hand now? Here we go.

After a puff of smoke (in the interval after the exhale and before the next inhale), You perform our Puff Triangle image as we practiced: "puff, and puff, and puff, and nothing". When you finish performing the image, you look at the cigarette in your hand. The look at the cigarette after the image is necessary, because that's what links the image to the smoking.

What's the frequency you should do this practice? With about a quarter of the cigarettes you smoke a day. When you reached smoking up to 4 cigarettes a day, do it with at least 1 cigarette. Three hits per cigarette is enough. You can do more, but don't do less.

The result.
As mentioned in the opening, according to our method you should always smoke as much as you want and whenever you want. While maintaining this core principle, quite quickly you will reduce your smoking to up to 5 cigarettes a day. What will happen is that you will start to postpone more and more cigarettes, while saying to yourself: "I don't feel like it now, later.4" At next step, you will find yourself postponing cigarettes all day long, and will be left with 1-2 cigarettes. As you progress, you will eventually lose your desire for it too – You will finally break your daily dependency.

Two common misconceptions during the recovery process.
Quite quickly, the amount of daily smoking decreases significantly, and you may feel that it's because the practice during the smoking simply disturbs you enjoy the smoking. It's not true, it's the assimilation of the image that does the work. If you want to prove it, decide not to perform any practice for the next 24 hours, and you will see that the number of cigarettes you smoke remains as low as before.

When you reach smoking a few cigarettes a day, you could feel that the practice has exhausted itself. That the meaning of the image has been fully assimilated, and there is no point in continuing it, since it no longer brings benefit. It's not true, it's simply that now the progress rate is slower. As long as you smoke on a daily basis, you still have a way to go. You are a few weeks away from reaching the target as we defined it: smoking up to 5 cigarettes a week. The number 5 is not coincidental, it's a proof that you have broken your daily dependency.

If we have already reached such a low desire to smoke, then why smoke at all?
The truth is that this is a completely personal question. Of course, if you have already reached such a point, it's very easy to end cigarettes forever. The thing is, since smoking a few cigarettes a week no longer poses a danger (neither in terms of health nor in terms of returning to smoke as before), an honest question arises: why not enjoy the advantage?

I myself buy 4 cigarettes every Monday, and use them during the week in some points. Where can it fit? Adding a cigarette to a can of beer is fun. A cigarette speeds up metabolism, and sometimes there's nothing like a cigarette to open a morning constipation. Smoking a cigarette after a few days without smoking is also nice, because it really makes your head spin. And Even just to have the opportunity to join a group of smokers for an occasional cigarette, without feeling like you've committed a crime - is a healthy thing in life.

Bottom line, if you're not an athlete or a pregnant mother - a cigarette here and there isn't such a bad idea...

Prestanite da pušite
pomoću Čarobne metode

Uvod
Kada i zašto?
Ovu metodu razvio sam tokom trogodišnjeg putovanja oko svijeta. To je situacija u kojoj je slobodnog vremena napretek, ponude opijata dolaze sa svih strana i u svim oblicima, a novac ne predstavlja prepreku. Bio mi je potreban alat koji će me zaštititi od te opasnosti. Ovo znanje dijelim po prvi put i nadam se da će vam biti korisno. Ova knjižica posvećena je isključivo cigaretama.

Šta ovu metodu čini jedinstvenom?
Kod svih postojećih metoda za prestanak pušenja polazi se od ideje da od sada zauvijek prestajemo da pušimo. Pretpostavlja se da, nakon što ostavimo pušenje, svaka cigareta koju zapalimo predstavlja opasnost. To je opasnost da se vrlo brzo vratimo staroj navici i počnemo da pušimo kao ranije. U čemu je problem? U tome što želja za pušenjem ne nestaje. Zato svaka cigareta koju od tada zapalimo može vrlo brzo ponovo rasplamsati vatru i vratiti nas pušenju.

Kod naše metode čitav pristup je suprotan. Mi nećemo prestati da pušimo – smanjićemo i na kraju ugasiti želju za pušenjem. Prema Čarobnoj metodi, treba da pušite onoliko koliko želite i kad god to želite. Međutim, ta želja će postajati sve manja, dok na kraju ne postane toliko slaba da više neće predstavljati problem. Primjenom ove metode relativno brzo ćete doći do toga da pušite najviše 3 cigarete dnevno, a kasnije i svega do 5 cigareta sedmično. Nećete morati da ulažete nikakav poseban napor da se zadržite na toj količini, jer će to biti upravo onoliko koliko ćete željeti da pušite – ni više ni manje. Zapravo, kada vaša žudnja za pušenjem oslabi, moći ćete da pušite potpuno slobodno. Mala količina cigareta koju ćete možda poželjeti tokom sedmice više neće predstavljati opasnost – ni po vaše zdravlje, ni u smislu povratka starim navikama i nekadašnjem intenzitetu pušenja. Upravo ćete izgubiti privlačnost koju pušenje ima za vas, pa poneka cigareta više neće biti prijetnja, jer nikada neće dovesti do toga da ponovo počnete da pušite više od toga.
Teorijski dio
Zašto pušimo?
Iz ugla pušača, razloga ima mnogo: već je navikao na pušenje, zavisan je od nikotina, ono ga opušta, cigarete predstavljaju njegove pauze tokom dana, voli da puši i tako dalje.

Sve su to tačni razlozi, ali su izraženi veoma blago. Suštinski razlog naše vezanosti za pušenje mnogo je manje prijatan za prihvatiti. Pušimo zato što nam cigareta služi kao štit od svakodnevice. A od čega nam je svakodnevno potrebna zaštita? Od brojnih neprijatnih emocija, kao što su brige, razočaranja, frustracije, poniženja, stres i mnoge druge. Kada se suočimo s takvim emocionalnim pritiskom i osjećajem unutrašnje zagušenosti, javlja se potreba za "vazduhom". Za pušača, cigareta predstavlja upravo taj predah. Ona donosi osjećaj oslobađanja od emocionalne zagušenosti koja se do tog trenutka nakupila.

Iluzija zaštite.
Ljepota osjećaja zaštite je u tome što on ne mora biti stvaran – dovoljno je da vjerujemo u njega. Zapravo, postali smo pravi stručnjaci za stvaranje izmišljenih odbrambenih mehanizama.

Evo jednog lijepog primjera koliko lako stvaramo takve mehanizme: Prije nekoliko godina držao sam predavanje pred velikim auditorijumom, a sama situacija izazivala mi je veliku tremu i stres. Zato sam ispred sebe postavio sto, sjeo iza njega i započeo predavanje. Iako taj sto, koji me je fizički odvajao od publike, nije pružao nikakvu stvarnu zaštitu, osjećao sam se znatno opuštenije. Tada sam odlučio da nešto isprobam. Ustao sam, udaljio se od stola i počeo slobodno da se krećem po bini, zamišljajući da se na njenom rubu nalazi ograda. Nećete vjerovati koliko je to dobro funkcionisalo. Sama pomisao na ogradu koja me odvaja od publike pružala mi je isti osjećaj sigurnosti kao i sto. Na kraju krajeva, kakva je razlika između stvarnog stola i zamišljene ograde, kada ni jedno ni drugo nemaju nikakvu stvarnu vrijednost zaštite?

To su iluzije zaštite – oblici poricanja. To su izmišljeni odbrambeni mehanizmi u koje biramo da vjerujemo kako bismo se osjećali zaštićeno od određene stvarnosti.

Pušenje je još jedna takva iluzija zaštite.
Lako je dokazati da je cigareta samo iluzija zaštite i da njena navodna vrijednost nema stvarno uporište, jer je cigareta veoma slabo sredstvo. Osoba koja svakodnevno puši gotovo da i ne osjeća nikakav poseban efekat dok puši. Ako je pušenje toliko neprimjetno, onda je jasno da je njegova emocionalna vrijednost uglavnom plod naše mašte. Kako cigareta može da me zaštiti od bilo čega ako je njeno djelovanje jedva primjetno? To je u potpunoj suprotnosti s alkoholom i drogama, koje su nesumnjivo mnogo snažnije supstance i čije se dejstvo jasno osjeća. Ali za ove momke imamo mnogo smješnije šale nego za cigarete. Međutim, danas ćemo se zadržati na temi pušenja...

Trik zavisnika od cigareta.
Kada srušimo iluziju zaštite koju vezujemo za cigarete, one odmah gube svoju privlačnost. A šta je to što treba da usvojimo i zaista razumijemo? Vrlo jednostavno: o cigaretama treba da shvatimo da, zapravo, pušimo ništa. Vučemo dim i vučemo dim i vučemo dim, ali osjećaja nema. Kada zaista usvojite činjenicu da pušite nešto čije dejstvo jedva osjećate, cigarete počinju da gube svoju privlačnost. Drugim riječima, vaša zavisnost počinje da se urušava.

Za sada sve izgleda dobro, ali postoji samo jedan problem: svi to već znaju! Svaki pušač zna da je cigareta supstanca čije je dejstvo jedva primjetno. Pa ipak, puši tokom cijelog dana. Drugim riječima, cigarete ne gube svoju snažnu privlačnost, iako zna da su potpuno nemoćne.

Kako dolazi do toga? Zbog mentalnog trika koji primjenjujemo kako bismo održali svoj izmišljeni štit. Znamo da kažemo da su cigarete neprimjetne kada se to od nas traži, ali smo to znanje odvojili od samih cigareta – ono nije postalo asocijacija koju vezujemo za njih. Kada biste, dok pušite cigaretu, zaista bili svjesni da je jedva osjećate, ona bi vam odmah izgubila svoju snažnu privlačnost. Međutim, pošto ta spoznaja nije istinski usvojena, već trenutak kasnije potpuno ćete je zaboraviti.

Isti trik koristimo i kada gledamo filmove. Uopšteno gledano, znamo da film nije stvaran, ali to ne znamo dok ga gledamo. Ako bih bio potpuno uživljen u neki triler, a vi mi za tijelo koje upravo vidim na ekranu rekli: "Znaš, ovaj čovjek zapravo nije mrtav. On je samo glumac. Živ je i diše, a čim režiser kaže: ‘Rez!’, svi će ustati i nastaviti dalje." Kako bih reagovao? Naravno da bih vam se nasmijao: "Znam to, genijalče, ali zašto mi kvariš film?"

Problem svih problema.
Dakle, želimo da pušenju pridružimo novu asocijaciju – beznačajnost cigarete. U čemu je problem? U tome što je to nešto što uopšte ne želimo da uradimo, a sve u vezi s tim aktivno poričemo. Zapravo, tu laž smo stvorili namjerno i uporno je održavamo, jer upravo ona gradi naš željeni izmišljeni odbrambeni mehanizam – cigarete i pušenje koji nam služe kao štit od svakodnevice. Pitanje od milion dolara glasi: kako to možemo da uradimo? Kako da razbijemo tu iluziju zaštite, koju tako snažno poričemo i koja nam je toliko dragocjena?

Čarobni štapić.
Nije problem to uraditi. Međutim, da bismo to postigli, ne možemo se oslanjati na verbalno razmišljanje. U čemu je problem s verbalnim razmišljanjem? Ono je veoma jedinstveno jer možemo da mislimo riječima, a da pritom ne razmišljamo o njihovom značenju.

Evo jednog primjera. Sigurno vam je poznata situacija iz škole, kraj je dana, već ste umorni i iscrpljeni, odjednom nastavnik počne da diktira velikom brzinom. Šta tada radimo? Pišemo, a da gotovo ništa ne razumijemo od onoga što nastavnik govori. Kako je to moguće? Tada riječi imaju samo svoj zvučni oblik, bez sloja značenja. Zato možemo da ih zapisujemo, ali ne možemo da razumijemo ono što zapravo znače.

Istu pojavu možemo primijetiti i prilikom čitanja. Ponekad, kada nam je dosadno ili smo jednostavno umorni, možemo pročitati čitav pasus, a da pritom ne razumijemo gotovo ništa od onoga što smo pročitali. Ipak, pročitali smo cijeli tekst. Drugim riječima, prošli smo kroz sve riječi i mislili ih, ali bez njihovog sloja značenja.

Pošto je povezivanje beznačajnosti cigarete s našim pušenjem nešto što ne želimo i što poričemo, ako budemo pokušali da to usvojimo putem verbalnog razmišljanja, iz toga će proizaći veoma malo stvarnog značenja. Zašto se to dešava? Zato što, kada je neko razmišljanje nepoželjno, um vrlo brzo odvaja riječi od njihovog značenja. Na taj način nas štiti od informacija koje ne želimo da prihvatimo. Međutim, za naš cilj upravo moramo da dopremo do sloja značenja, jer se u njemu nalazi razumijevanje. Upravo tako usvajamo novu asocijaciju i povezujemo je s predmetom, odnosno sa cigaretom.

Zato nam je potreban čarobni štapić, a to je vizuelno razmišljanje. Za razliku od verbalnog razmišljanja, koje može postojati bez značenja, vizuelno razmišljanje uvijek dolazi zajedno sa značenjem. Zato svaki prikaz koji stvorimo u umu automatski sadrži i svoje značenje. Na taj način možemo povezati svoje pušenje sa činjenicom da zapravo pušimo nešto beznačajno – putem prikaza. To će obaviti posao, i to iznenađujuće brzo.
Tehnički dio
Odabrana tehnika.
Dakle, želimo da činjenicu da je cigareta gotovo neprimjetna pretvorimo u reprezentativan prikaz.

Postoji nekoliko načina da se to uradi. Moj omiljeni je čisto vizuelno razmišljanje, koje koristi zamišljene slike, pa čak i kratke scene. Međutim, pošto je ovo knjižica, opredijeliću se za način koji je najjednostavniji za primjenu. Efekat je isti, samo je pristup nešto manje živopisan i zanimljiv.

Nećemo koristiti zamišljenu sliku, već zamišljeni oblik. Oblik predstavlja svojevrstan trik. Omogućava nam da koristimo riječi, a da pritom ne izgube svoje značenje. Kako? Tako što riječi ugrađujemo kao uporišne tačke unutar zamišljenog oblika. Na taj način ugrađene riječi postaju sastavni dio prikaza. Zahvaljujući tome, možemo ih koristiti bez opasnosti da vrlo brzo ostanu bez značenja. Hajde da ovaj trik oživimo.

Izvođenje našeg prikaza.
Prvo nam je potrebno ime za naš prikaz: Trougao dimova. Poruka Trougla dimova je sljedeća: vučemo dim i vučemo dim i vučemo dim iz cigarete, ali ništa se ne dešava – nema nikakvog osjećaja.

Kako izgleda naš prikaz? To je jednostavan trougao. Na njegovom vrhu ispisana je riječ "dim", na desnom uglu njegove osnove riječi "i dim", a na lijevom uglu osnove takođe riječi "i dim". Unutar trougla ispisane su riječi "i ništa".

Kako je zamišljate? U svojoj mašti krećete se kroz trougao od tačke do tačke. Počinjete od vrha i kažete "dim", zatim prelazite na desni ugao osnove i kažete "i dim", potom na lijevi ugao osnove i kažete "i dim", a onda odlazite u središte trougla i kažete "i ništa". Naravno, riječi izgovarate u sebi – one su nečujne.

Obratite pažnju da upravo kretanje kroz prikaz, od tačke do tačke, sprječava riječi da izgube svoje značenje. Zato je to zamišljeno kretanje kroz prikaz neophodno. Bez tog zamišljenog kretanja kroz naš trougao, zapravo bismo prešli iz vizuelnog razmišljanja u verbalno razmišljanje.

Sada vi pokušajte. Recite u sebi: "dim, i dim, i dim, i ništa", dok se u mašti krećete od tačke do tačke kroz naš Trougao dimova. Počinjete na vrhu ("dim"), zatim prelazite na desni ugao osnove ("i dim"), potom na lijevi ugao osnove ("i dim"), a završavate u središtu trougla ("i ništa").

Ako ne znate odakle da počnete, jednostavno zatvorite oči. Najprije pokušajte sa zatvorenim očima. Zatim uradite isto sa otvorenim očima.

Ponovite ovo 5 do 10 puta zaredom kako biste uvježbali tehniku. Pokušajte to nekoliko puta i bez našeg Trougla dimova, bez kretanja kroz njega, i osjetite razliku. Primijetite koliko lako riječi gube svoje značenje kada izostane kretanje unutar našeg prikaza.

Sada naš prikaz moramo povezati sa našim pušenjem.
Nakon što ste naučili kako da zamišljate naš Trougao dimova, prelazimo na ono što je zaista važno: povezujemo naš prikaz sa samim činom pušenja. Jeste li upravo zapalili cigaretu? Da li vam je cigareta sada u ruci? Krećemo.

Nakon povlačenja dima (u periodu nakon izdisaja, a prije sljedećeg udisaja), izvodite naš prikaz Trougla dimova, onako kako smo ga uvježbali: "dim, i dim, i dim, i ništa". Kada završite sa izvođenjem prikaza, pogledajte cigaretu u svojoj ruci. Pogled na cigaretu nakon izvođenja prikaza je neophodan, jer upravoto povezuje prikaz ne samo sa činom pušenja, već i sa samom cigaretom.

Koliko često treba da radite ovu vježbu? Radite je uz približno četvrtinu cigareta koje popušite tokom dana. Kada dođete do toga da pušite najviše 4 cigarete dnevno, radite je uz najmanje 1 cigaretu. Tri ponavljanja po cigareti su dovoljna. Možete ih uraditi i više, ali nemojte manje od toga.

Rezultat.
Kao što je pomenuto u uvodu, prema našoj metodi uvijek treba da pušite onoliko koliko želite i kad god to želite. Uz pridržavanje ovog osnovnog principa, veoma brzo ćete smanjiti pušenje na najviše 5 cigareta dnevno. Ono što će se dešavati jeste da ćete sve češće odlagati cigarete, govoreći sebi: "Ne puši mi se sada, kasnije." U sljedećoj fazi primijetićete da odlažete cigarete tokom čitavog dana, pa će vam ostati svega 1-2. Kako budete napredovali, na kraju ćete izgubiti želju i za njima – konačno ćete prekinuti svoju svakodnevnu zavisnost.

Dvije česte zablude tokom procesa oporavka.
Količina cigareta koju dnevno pušite smanjiće se prilično brzo, pa biste mogli pomisliti da je razlog tome to što vam vježba tokom pušenja jednostavno kvari uživanje u cigareti. To nije tačno. Posao zapravo obavlja usvajanje prikaza. Ako želite da se u to uvjerite, odlučite da naredna 24 sata ne radite nikakvu vježbu. Vidjećete da će broj cigareta koje popušite ostati jednako nizak kao i prije.

Kada dođete do toga da pušite svega nekoliko cigareta dnevno, mogli biste steći utisak da je vježba iscrpila svoj učinak. Da je značenje prikaza u potpunosti usvojeno i da nema smisla nastavljati s njom jer više ne donosi nikakvu korist. To nije tačno. Jednostavno je riječ o tome da je napredak sada sporiji. Sve dok pušite svakodnevno, pred vama je još dio puta. Nekoliko sedmica vas dijeli od cilja koji smo definisali – da pušite najviše 5 cigareta sedmično. Broj 5 nije slučajno odabran. On predstavlja dokaz da ste prekinuli svoju svakodnevnu zavisnost.

Ako smo već došli do toga da je želja za pušenjem toliko mala, zašto onda uopšte pušiti?
Istina je da je to potpuno lično pitanje. Naravno, ako ste već došli do te tačke, veoma je lako zauvijek ostaviti cigarete. Međutim, pošto pušenje nekoliko cigareta sedmično više ne predstavlja opasnost (ni po zdravlje, niti u smislu povratka nekadašnjem načinu pušenja), nameće se jedno sasvim opravdano pitanje: zašto ne iskoristiti tu prednost?

Ja lično svakog ponedjeljka kupim 4 cigarete i rasporedim ih tokom sedmice za određene prilike. Uz limenku piva, cigareta može biti prijatna. Cigareta ubrzava metabolizam, a ponekad nema ničeg djelotvornijeg od cigarete da pomogne kod jutarnjeg zatvora. Cigareta nakon nekoliko dana bez pušenja takođe može biti prijatna, jer zaista izazove vrtoglavicu. Čak i sama mogućnost da se povremeno pridružite grupi pušača i zapalite cigaretu, bez osjećaja kao da ste počinili zločin – predstavlja zdravu stvar u životu.

U suštini, ako niste sportista ili trudnica, poneka cigareta s vremena na vrijeme i nije tako loša ideja...

About Me
I was 16 when the "bug" first got into me. I had a huge and inexplicable urge to explore my spirituality in a somewhat psychic way. The idea was to create a dictionary of all my feelings. While in a normal state you live your daily life, feel it and react to it - I lived my daily life, felt it, and constantly focused on each feeling I had, in order to observe and explore it. Over the course of 3 years, I built a dictionary that included over 300 different feelings, divided into categories and subcategories, and each feeling was characterized by a set of several attributes. It was the opening chord of an obsession that began at such a young age, and has actually lived with me to this day.

The years passed, I turned 22, and I realized that in our material world, there is no livelihood from such direction. And if I want to continue to engage with these "materials", I must find a profession that will provide me well, in order to leave me maximum time for my mania. And in Israel, such a profession is software development of course. I studied the field and entered the industry at the age of 25. At first, I worked full-time jobs, but as I specialized, I was able to move to part-time jobs, and also do freelance work. Then I basically lived two lives, half a week as a software developer, and half a week as a "lunatic" exploring spirituality - while each half denies the existence of the other, because they contradict each other in every way.

At the age of 37, I had accumulated around 5,000 pages of psychological knowledge, which you will not find in any psychological or spiritual book. The problem with this enormous amount of knowledge, was that I couldn't find any order in it. Then the miracle happened: like a hand of God touching me and revealing me a secret, I found myself in the next 3 years, packing 5,000 pages of scattered knowledge, into 100 pages of a book that looks like a picture, literally a painting designed by God, showing how the human mind works.

I published this book on my 40th birthday, not forgetting the kid who started it all. I did it along with a decision that my double life is over. I abandoned the computer's world, in order to dedicate the rest of my life solely to my childhood dream. Then I went on a world trip for several years. I started in the Far East, from there I continued to Australia, then South America, and ended in Europe, where I found the love of my life - my village "Crna Gora". On this trip, I developed The Magic Method of rehabbing from any addiction, based on a small part of the knowledge I packed in my book. I am sharing it for the first time. Any activity related to it is voluntary.
